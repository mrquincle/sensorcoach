SensorCoach


"Ieder nadeel heb zijn voordeel"

Johan Cruijff aan het woord

This website is meant for coaches that want to help people in improving their health through sporting. On the moment the social media can be of a big help. Everybody knows how hard it is to keep sporting and adhere to your good intentions. Sports schools thrive on subscriptions of people that bail out after the first few visits. We need our friends and their feedback to keep working hard! We need schedules that are in touch with whom we are. If everbody in the Netherlands is sad because it is now raining for the third day in a row, it might not be the best time to schedule that run. By publishing encouragements, reminders, goals, compliments, besides just results online, friends can engage in what we want to achieve: a healthy life and healthy habits. Of course, it might very well be counterproductive to just put online everything with respect to our intentions and achievements with respect to sporting.

The challenge is to come up with actual coaching strategies that are tailored to the individual. This online, virtual trainer will need to learn what works for one person and not for the other. The trainee will send data with respect to her sporting using contemporary smart devices, such as smartphones and fittness add-ons to the internet. The virtual trainer will respond with schedules, motivating messages, etc. and have the choice to do so in private via the application versus in public via social media. Over time the trainer, trainee, and her friends will become better and better the best healthy, encouraging atmosphere possible.
